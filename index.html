Introducing My Site

I got into coding and Web Development because I am chronically ill. I tried to go the traditional route; ie go to college, graduate, get a job, but I wound up getting so sick that I had to drop out. I was afraid that I would never be able to follow through any sort of traditional education due to my poor health. But, as I got older and began to start my life and my family, I knew that I needed to do something more and support myself and them. Enter Lambda School and my journey into the coding world. I started off in Data Science, but soon realized that my heart is that of an artist. I needed creativity in some way to be happy in my future career. My journey into Web Development and coding overall may be short at the moment, but I have truly learned so much. I am proud of what I have accomplished so far, and what I will accomplish in the future.
